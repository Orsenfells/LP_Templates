Dental Insurance Accepted

For patients with dental insurance, the initial oral examination, radiographs (x-rays) and cleaning are usually covered 100% with no out-of-pocket expenses. There are exceptions with certain insurance companies and specific coverage is dependent on the plan that your employer purchased for you. For procedures that require a copayment, we look up your insurance coverage, estimate your copayment responsibilities and inform you before proceeding with treatment.
