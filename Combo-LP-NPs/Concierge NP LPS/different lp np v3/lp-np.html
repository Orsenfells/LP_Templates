Why Trust Our Prosthodontists

Prosthodontists focus on rebuilding the bite and replacing the teeth with traditional lifelike restorations such as dentures and dental implants. What makes us unique is our minimal invasive restoration techniques such as veneers and ceramic onlays. Prosthodontists undergo years of additional training beyond dental school to do so.

Advanced Prosthodontics & Implant Esthetics is committed to providing superior prosthodontic and cosmetic dental care to our patients in all Southwest Florida. Dr. Binns & Dr. Aquino see our patients as individuals and create a preventive, restorative and maintenance care plan that is specific to each patient’s dental needs.
