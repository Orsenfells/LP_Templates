We understand. Taking care of your teeth and gums is not always your highest priority. In fact, many people do not like going to the dentist. That is why we encourage you to give Cosmetic & Restorative Dentistry of the Keys a try.

We are different, and we strive to make every visit amazing. Our team starts with the highest quality safety standards and a super-clean, modern office. Dental insurance is welcome, and we do all we can to make high-quality dentistry affordable. We offer same-day appointments, the latest technology and a relaxing atmosphere.

We want you to love visiting Cosmetic & Restorative Dentistry of the Keys.

It All Starts with You

You will be amazed at the friendliness and thoroughness of all we do. With a passion for helping people smile, our team will provide you with a complete assessment of your teeth, gums and jaw. We will take all necessary x-rays and devote the time to answer all your questions.

If a dental emergency is the reason for your visit, our first priority is to get you out of pain.

If you are visiting for the first time as a new patient, you will receive a thorough evaluation of the health of your mouth. We will screen for oral cancer and take a video tour of your teeth and gums. Also, we will evaluate the health of any existing crowns, inlays, onlays, dentures or partial dentures.
