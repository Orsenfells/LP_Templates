Get to Know Dr. Walker

The success of our practice is founded on the great relationships we enjoy with our patients — you! And, our amazing team makes these relationships possible.

With this in mind, we wanted to take a moment to help you get to know our team as people.

They are so much more than dentists, hygienists, assistants, and front desk associates — they are people just like you with many of the same interests.

That's what makes a relationship stronger — and what makes it a pleasure to see you smile.

Before graduating from high school in Astoria, OR, Dr. Walker was a photographer for his high school yearbook, had his own paper route, and worked at McDonalds. Fortunately, he still had time to do something really cool. He volunteered at the Wildlife Rehab Center and focused on rehabbing bald eagles.

Dr. Walker served in the U.S. Marine Corps for ten years with multiple deployments overseas; including Japan, Southern Philippines, and East Africa. He provided intelligence support to special forces units, such as the Green Berets and Navy Seals. Also, he was a black belt martial arts instructor and airborne qualified paratrooper.

As a Super Dad, Dr. Walker enjoys drawing, building Legos, playing at the park, soccer, swimming, traveling, attending car shows, and monster jams. He and his son also love doing science experiments, walking Teddy, making coffee, eating steak, and playing racecar video games.

While stationed in Oahu, Dr. Walker adopted Teddy when he was 6 months old. Teddy is a Maltese with true Hawaiian spirit who has traveled all over the country as part of their family.
