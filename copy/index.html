We understand. Taking care of your teeth and gums is not always your highest priority. In fact, we realize most people do not even like going to dentist. That is why we encourage you to give Meadowbrook Dentistry a try.

We are different, and we strive to make every visit amazing. We start with the highest safety standards and a super-clean, modern office. Dental insurance is welcome, and we do all we can to make high-quality affordable. We offer same-day appointments, the latest technology and a relaxing atmosphere.

We want you to love visiting Meadowbrook Dentistry.

We Are Here for You

You will be amazed at the friendliness and thoroughness of all we do. With a passion for helping people smile, our team will provide you with a complete assessment of your teeth, gums and jaw. We will take all necessary x-rays and devote the time to answer all your questions and provide you with treatment options that meet your goals.

If a dental emergency is the reason for your visit, our first priority is to get you out of pain.

If you are visiting for the first time as a new patient, you will receive a thorough evaluation of the health of your mouth. We will screen for oral cancer and take a tour of your teeth and gums. Also, we will evaluate the health of any existing crowns, fillings, root canals, implants, and dentures.

Dental Insurance Welcome

We realize the importance of taking care of your teeth, and we welcome dental insurance as a partner in making exceptional dentistry affordable. If you are fortunate enough to have PPO dental insurance, call us. We are an in-network provider for many of the leading plans, and our team will work with you to maximize your benefits.

Note: If you do not see your plan, do not worry. Call. We welcome other plans.

No Insurance? No Worries!

If you do not have dental insurance or would like to consider a better option, give us a call. We are proud to help make dentistry more affordable by offering our Dental Savings Plan. For a low monthly fee, individuals and families can receive their routine, preventative care along with significant discounts on other services. No limits. No waiting periods. No surprises. In many cases, it is simpler, easier – and less expensive – than dental insurance.
