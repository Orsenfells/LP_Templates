How Invisalign Works

Starting with a digital model of your mouth, we are able to utilize the latest technology to fabricate a series of clear aligners. Used in sequence, these thin, plastic devices move your teeth into their ideal positions – quickly and painlessly. Your entire treatment is based on a computer simulation of your “perfect smile”, and we can achieve amazing results comfortably and predictably. Invisalign is an ideal solution for kids, teens and adults with routine orthodontic challenges, and everyone benefits from fewer trips to the dentist. Most importantly, fewer visits means time and money savings for you and your family.

Orthodontics
for All Ages

Braces aren’t just for children - Everyone needs straight teeth! For children, the main goal of orthodontics is to prevent problematic overcrowding, misalignment, uneven bites and non-nutritive habits when it’s most treatable. While most adults look to orthodontics for cosmetic reasons, straight teeth can reduce oral complications as you age. At Benchmark Family Dental and Orthodontics, we provide the highest-quality, safest and personal orthodontic treatment available to patients aged 7 to 87. Whoever in your family needs orthodontic care, you can step into our office with peace of mind, because we are more than ready to give them a gorgeous smile!
